About me

Welcome! I'm Martha. I grew up between France and Spain and I am now based in Vancouver, BC. My interest for web development really started to sparkle in 2018 when I wanted to help my parents build a website for their pizzeria in Spain, which I originally did using Bootsrap. I seldomly followed online courses and I really enjoyed it. I finally took the leap to seriously tackle programming by enrolling into the Front-End Development program at BCIT. I am extremely excited about this new journey!
